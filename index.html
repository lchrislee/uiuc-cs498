Gas and Diesel Cars

When looking at what cars to buy, it's important to compare how efficient the car is while it travels. While gas and diesel cars run on different fuel, they both have similar designs and contain a combustion engine. The number of cylinders in the engine help determine the maximum amount of fuel that can be continously introduced to the engine. While the number of cylinders is not a direct indicator of fuel consumption or maximum speed, it definitely plays a major role.
